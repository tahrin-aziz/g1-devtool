Extract the (fractional) segments from solvedProfile between t0 and t1 and save them in this profile. The displacement between t0 and t1 must match s[0] and s[7] of this profile i.e. the t0 and t7 must have been obtained by computing crossing times for s[0] and s[7] on solvedProfile.
