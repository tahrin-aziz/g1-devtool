Detailed Description

A method to allow after-pick, pre-place alignment of parts on the nozzle. Bottom vision is an implementation of this interface, but other implementations could include laser alignment or pit alignment.

PartAlignmentOffset org.openpnp.spi.PartAlignment.findOffsets ( Part  part,
BoardLocation  boardLocation,
Location  placementLocation,
Nozzle  nozzle 
) throws Exception

Perform the part alignment operation. The method must return a Location containing the offsets on the nozzle of the aligned part and these offsets will be applied by the JobProcessor. The offsets returned may be zero if the alignment process results in physical alignment of the part as in the case of pit based alignment. The Z portion of the Location is ignored.
