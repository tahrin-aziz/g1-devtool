static

Finds circles of the given min and maxDiameter, no less than minDistance apart by capturing an image from the given Camera. Results are returned as a List of Location where X and Y represent the center of the circle, Z is that of the Camera and rotation is the diameter of the circle found. Results are returned sorted by distance from the Camera Location in ascending order.
