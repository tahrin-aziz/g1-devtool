Detailed Description

Backlash compensation is used to counter any looseness or play in the mechanical linkages of the given axis.
When the actuator reverses the direction of travel, there is often a moment where nothing happens, because the slack from the belt or lash/play from the lead/ball screw needs to be taken up, before mechanical force can again be transmitted. Note that backlash is often unavoidable, otherwise friction will be too high or lubrication impossible.

org.openpnp.machine.reference.axis.ReferenceControllerAxis.BacklashCompensationMethod.OneSidedPositioning

Backlash compensation is applied by always moving to the end position from one side. The backlash offset does not need to be very precise, i.e. it can be larger than the actual backlash and the machine will still end up in the correct precise position.
The machine always needs to perform an extra move.
