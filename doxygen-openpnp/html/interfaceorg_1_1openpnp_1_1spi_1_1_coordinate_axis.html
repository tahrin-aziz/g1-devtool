◆ getCoordinate()

double org.openpnp.spi.CoordinateAxis.getCoordinate ( )
Returns
The coordinate that was last sent to the MotionPlanner either through a moveTo() or other operations, such as homing.

This coordinate may not yet be sent to the driver and even less likely reflect the physical machine position. Only after a waitForCompletion() on the MotionPlanner, can you be sure that the coordinate is in sync with the machine.

The coordinate is always in AxesLocation.getUnits() i.e. in Millimeters.

Implemented in org.openpnp.spi.base.AbstractCoordinateAxis.
